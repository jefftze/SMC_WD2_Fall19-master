Brewery, Taproom and Kitchen

Lomita's own local Microbrewery!

Burnin Daylight Brewing Company is a family owned and operated brewpub supported with a couple of friends, and two friendly brew dogs Dublin and Mac.

Brewery

All can releases are 15th and last day of the Month

ALL can releases are announced day before release. We do not give this information to anyone ahead of time. If you are wondering if we have more cans for sale the following day, please call to find out.

Taproom

Taste the local Microbrews of South Bay

A space within the brewery where guests learn about microbrewing or taste and purchase beers created at the brewery and other partnered microbrews in the South Bay area.

Kitchen

Great food with your Microbrew!

Sit and enjoy where the food is great, the conversation easy, the drinks are plentiful, and most of all, you are among friends. It’s a place you truly can’t wait to return to.
